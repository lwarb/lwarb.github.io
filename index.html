Lwarb Format
LWARB is a casual format that me and some friend recently cooked up. The basic idea is that each player picks (randomly or not) one enchantment from this list, and it starts in play. Each player starts with 30 life if LWARB

FORMAT RULES
1.0 - At the start of the game, each player chooses one of the options.
1x Confusion In the Ranks
1x Eye of the Storm
1x Intruder Alarm
1x Knowledge Pool
1x Bludgeon Brawl
1x Elkin Lair
1x Field of Dreams
1x Revelation
1x Grip of Chaos
1x Infernal Genesis
*x Mind's Dilation (each player gets 1 Mind's Dilation under their control.)
*x Sunbird's Invocation (each player gets 1 Sunbird's Invocation under their control.)
*x Possibility Storm (each player gets 1 Possibility Storm under their control.)
Playtesting List
1x Hive Mind
1x Ley Line
1x Upwelling
1x Mist of Stagnation
1x Death Pits of Rath
1x Cowardice
*x Maelstrom Nexus (each player gets 1 Maelstrom Nexus under their control.)
*x Conjured Currency (each player gets 1 Conjured Currency under their control.)
*x Guild Feud (each player gets 1 Guild Feud under their control.)
*x Mindmoil (each player gets 1 Mindmoil under their control.)

1.1 - Each of these cards are Emblems and start on the battlefield.  They are all colorless. They are under no player's control, with exceptions.


1.2 - The triggers of these Emblems happen after draws, mulligans and other pre-game actions, but before the first player's turn.


1.3 - The selection of these emblems starts with the player going first, followed by the next player going around anticlockwise or clockwise.


1.4 - Eye of the Storm may not be chosen if Knowledge Pool has been selected and vice-versa.

2.0 - Lwarb is a constructed format. Decks are minimum 60 cards with no deck size limit other than you must be able to shuffle unassisted. Players start the game with 30 life points.
